'Stay Safe Online' Checklist for Indian College Students

[ ] Different passwords: Use different passwords for your bank/university and other platforms. 

[ ] App-Level Locks: Enable fingerprint or Face ID locks specifically for WhatsApp, Instagram, and UPI apps.

[ ] Revoke Permissions: Regularly delete third-party app access from your .edu.in or Google account.

[ ] Verify Identity: Call professors or friends directly if they send "urgent" money or data requests.

[ ] Avoid Modded Apps: Never download "Pro" or "Cracked" APKs from Telegram; they often contain spyware.

[ ] The 'PIN' Rule (UPI): Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. PINs are only for sending.

[ ] Set Limits (UPI): Lower your daily UPI transaction limit in your bank app to cap potential losses.

[ ] Data over Wi-Fi: Use mobile data instead of free campus/café Wi-Fi for banking or student portals.
