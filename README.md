Project Overview

As a Student Digital Ambassador at VIT Bhopal University, I have developed this portfolio to guide my peers through the digital world—from creating professional online profiles to communicating safely and responsibly. This repository documents my work across five modules, demonstrating my ability to use essential digital tools and maintain a secure online presence.


Repository Structure

This project is organized into specific folders for each task as per the project requirements:

report/: Full written Project Report in .pdf or .docx format.

task-1-presentation/: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic and brief description.

task-2-portfolio/: Screenshots of LinkedIn, GitHub, and ResearchGate profiles with reflection notes.

task-3-platforms/: HackerRank activity screenshots and Google Form quiz links.

task-4-email-etiquette/: Professional email drafts and the Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist.

task-5-cybercrime/: Cyberbullying Case Study and 'Stay Safe Online' Prevention Checklist.
