📖 Project Overview
As a Student Digital Ambassador at VIT Bhopal University, I have been tasked with building a Digital Literacy Portfolio to help my peers navigate the digital world. My role involves guiding batchmates in creating professional online profiles and communicating safely and responsibly. This repository serves as a comprehensive collection of five tasks that map directly to the course modules, showcasing my ability to use essential digital tools, practice safe internet habits, and maintain a high standard of professional etiquette. The project is designed to present my findings to a faculty committee and share useful resources with my batch.


📂 Repository Structure

This project is organized into specific folders for each task as per the project requirements:

report/: Full written Project Report in .pdf or .docx format.

task-1-presentation/: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic and brief description.

task-2-portfolio/: Screenshots of LinkedIn, GitHub, and ResearchGate profiles with reflection notes.

task-3-platforms/: HackerRank activity screenshots and Google Form quiz links.

task-5-cybercrime/: Cyberbullying Case Study and 'Stay Safe Online' Prevention Checklist.


🛠️ Module Summaries

1: Digital Literacy Awareness: 

For this module, I acted as a visual communicator by designing a one-page infographic using Canva. The goal was to help my batchmates understand the definition of digital literacy and why it is critical for their academic journey. The visual covers three key areas: useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, and professional online presence.

2: Professional Digital Portfolio:

In this module, I focused on building a foundational professional presence by setting up accounts on LinkedIn, GitHub, and ResearchGate. This involved creating a specialized GitHub profile README and detailing my educational background on LinkedIn. These platforms are essential for connecting with the academic community, employers, and internship coordinators.

3: Coding & Collaboration: 

This task emphasized technical familiarity with coding and cloud collaboration tools. I completed a beginner-level challenge on HackerRank to practice coding fundamentals and created a 5-question Google Form to assess the digital literacy awareness of my peers. This dual approach ensures I can guide others toward the right platforms for studying and practicing.

Quiz Link: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd78TgfNUj22MeZvWw6jiXhWoDeSw89LHz_AMPxhtDTHLqQdw/viewform

4: Professional Communication:

The focus here was on mastering professional communication and teaching these rules to others. I drafted formal emails to a professor for an extension and to a company for an internship, ensuring strict adherence to professional structure and tone. Additionally, I compiled a list of 5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use to prevent communication breakdowns.

5: Cybercrime Awareness:

As an ambassador, I researched cyber threats to help my peers recognize and avoid online dangers. This module includes a 200–250 word case study on Cyberbullying, detailing the crime's mechanics and consequences. It also features a 8-tip "Stay Safe Online" checklist specifically designed for Indian students, including UPI safety and official reporting channels like the 1930 helpline.
